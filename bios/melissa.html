Melissa Li

B.A. & Sc. Student

Melissa is an undergraduate student currently pursuing a Bachelors of Arts and Science in Cognitive Science at McGill University. She joined the ACT lab to research and design user experiences that enhance accessibility and usability of smart devices for older adults. Previously, she has worked at the Montreal Neurological Institute and the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Research (DSAIR) lab at Nanyang Technological University, where she focused on emotional learning tools for children with special needs. Currently, Melissa's interests lie at the intersection of design, psychology and HCI, with a particular enthusiasm for digital equity and accessibility.
