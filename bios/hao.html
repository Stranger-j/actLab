Short bio

Hao Ju is a Master's student with the School of Information Studies at McGill University. She received a B.Eng in Electrical Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Before joining ACT lab, Hao worked at City University of Hong Kong as a research assistant. Hao designs, builds, and explores the accessibility and usability of wearable devices and tangible interfaces. She is currently working on a foot-based interface for older adults.
