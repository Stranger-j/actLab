Taciana Pontual

Postdoctoral Researcher

Taciana is a lecturer and researcher at the Brazilian public university UFRPE, at the Computing Department (Departamento de Computação), in Recife, Pernambuco. In 2017, she was a post-doctoral researcher at the ACT-Lab at McGill University (School of Information Studies) – Montreal, in an AGE-WELL project on developing technologies to support the communication of older adults. She previously worked as a research officer at the London Knowledge Lab, and as an interaction designer at CESAR Recife. She graduated in Computer Science at CIn/UFPE, where she also did her master in tangible technologies for learning. Her PhD was on tangibles for intellectual disabilities, at the Institute of Education, University of London. Her research interests include: technologies for learning, technologies for learning disabilities, user experience, human-computer interaction, accessibility, teaching and learning of programming languages. She is passionate about her profession, has strong social beliefs and dreams of making the world a better place.
